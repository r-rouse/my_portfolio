# Projects

## Tax Receipt Generator

An interactive tool that shows how federal tax dollars are allocated based on salary, state, and tax year selections.

- **URL:** https://taxreceipt.netlify.app/
- **Technologies:** React, Data Viz, UI

## Whole Food Calories

A comprehensive nutrition tool for tracking calories and nutritional information for whole foods, helping users make informed dietary choices.

- **URL:** https://wholefoodcalories.com/
- **Technologies:** React, Nutrition, Data

## Retail Scraper

A web scraping tool for retail data analysis, enabling users to extract and analyze product information from various retail websites efficiently.

- **URL:** https://retailscraper.streamlit.app/
- **Technologies:** Streamlit, Python, Web Scraping

## San Jose Flat Tire Tracker

A TestFlight iOS app that allows users to tag locations where they got flat tires on their bikes in San Jose. The goal is to crowd-source data and lobby the city to sweep certain high-risk areas, making cycling safer for everyone.

- **Platform:** TestFlight iOS app
- **Technologies:** React Native, iOS, Crowdsourcing, Civic Tech
